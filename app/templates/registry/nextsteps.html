Why File Multiple Reports?
  • Documenting all the violations you encounter in a single report enables offenders to hide the scale of what is happening.
  • Submitting what you can finish in one sitting, and adding more reports later helps manage trauma and PTSD.
  • Submitting violations by type supports collective action and better enables us to track patterns of abuses.
  • Creating a report as the violations occur builds a timely record; you can choose to publish or file later when you feel ready.
