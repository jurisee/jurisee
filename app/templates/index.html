Juri See

A collective of professional human rights workers, lawyers, journalists, technologists, community organizers, and investigators organizing affected individuals; conducting in-depth investigations; and providing data-at-scale to enable evidence-driven reporting and transparency in our shared fight against civil court corruption.

Fighting to End Judicial Corruption

What We Do

Registry

We are collecting reports of corruption (which you can provide anonymously) and tracking official complaints to bring transparency and accountability to corrupt players and oversight bodies.

Data Driven Investigations

We are using data at scale, community reporting, and proven investigative methodology to trace criminal connections and patterns within the civil court ecosystem. It is time to bring needed transparency to this judicial branch of government, and readily identify the big players, cronyism, and hallmarks of corruption.

An API of public data, making sure publicly-owned data is public, is coming soon.

Community Led Actions

We are empowering individuals caught within the system to document and challenge abuses in their case, safely report misconduct, take collective action, help to track the big players of corruption, increase the effectiveness of court watching, and more.
